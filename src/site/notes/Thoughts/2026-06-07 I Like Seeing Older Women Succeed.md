## I Like Seeing Older Women Succeed

Have you ever noticed that young guys, young gals, and older guys are ubiquitous on places like YouTube, but it's almost impossible to find older women? As I've hit middle age, I've found that objectionable.

But, today I found a little YouTube channel called [Take 30 Plants](https://youtube.com/@take30plants). I wasn't looking for anything like it. It just popped up in my feed. The creator's name is Allison. She's a 60-year-old Gen-Xer from Scotland living on a small permaculture farm in France — by choice, she'll tell you.

I like her.

She's older than me, yes. A quiet, unassuming former-teacher with a penchant for speaking the truth without apology or adornment, but kindly. I think she's worth a watch.
